## Overview
LocalLoop is a hyperlocal e-commerce platform that connects users with nearby local shops, artisans, and small businesses.  
The platform focuses on **location-based discovery, fast fulfillment, and supporting local sellers**, with a lightweight smart assistant to simplify shopping.

---

## Problem Statement
- Local shops lack digital visibility and easy online selling tools.
- Customers want quick access to nearby products instead of long delivery times.
- Existing e-commerce platforms favor large sellers over local businesses.

---

## Proposed Solution
LocalLoop enables users to:
- Discover products from nearby sellers based on location.
- Shop from verified local businesses.
- Build carts quickly using a simple smart assistant.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Hyperlocal Marketplace (Core)
- Browse products by city/locality and category.
- Each product displays seller details and distance.
- Options for local delivery or store pickup.

### 2. Seller Dashboard
- Easy seller onboarding.
- Add, update, and manage products.
- View orders and basic insights.

### 3. Smart Cart Assistant (Lightweight)
- Helps users build carts based on budget or intent.
- Suggests relevant items from nearby sellers.
- Rule-based logic (no heavy AI dependency).

### 4. User & Order Management
- User authentication.
- Cart and order management.
- Order status tracking.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** SQLite3 with Prisma ORM
- **Deployment:** Vercel (frontend), Render (backend)
